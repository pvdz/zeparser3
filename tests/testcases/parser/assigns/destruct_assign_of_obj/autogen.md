## Input

### Cases

#### Bad cases

> `````js

> `````

> `````js
> ({...obj1,a} = foo)
> `````

> `````

> `````js
> ({...(a,b)} = foo)
> `````

> `````js
> ({...{}} = {})
> `````

#### Good cases

> `````js
> ({...(obj)} = foo)
> `````

> `````js
> ({...obj} = foo)
> `````

> `````js
> ({...obj.x} = foo)
> `````

> `````js
> ({...{}.x} = foo)
> `````

> `````js
> ({...[].x} = foo)
> `````
